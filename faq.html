FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What products do you offer?

Answer: We offer a range of high-quality men's clothing including coats, trousers, and ties.

What materials are your products made of?

Answer: Our products are made from a variety of premium materials, ensuring both comfort and style. For Coats, We normally uses wool or cashmere which offers warmth and a sophisticated look and for ties, we uses slik as our main materials and it offers a smooth, glossy appearance and lastly for trousers we mainly uses wool and also cotton.

Can I find sizing information for your products?

Answer: Yes, you can find sizing charts on each product page to help you choose the right size.

Can I purchase items separately, or do you offer bundles with a coat, trousers, and tie together?

Answer: We only have the option to purchase items individually. However, we may also offer discounts or sets in the future.

What sets your clothing apart from other brands?

Answer: Our clothing is known for its combination of style, quality, and affordability. We pay attention to the latest fashion trends and use durable materials to ensure that our customers look and feel their best in our products.

What is the shipping cost and delivery time for my order?

Answer: Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on your location and the shipping method selected during checkout.

Do you offer custom tailoring or alterations for a perfect fit?

Answer: Currently, we do not offer custom tailoring or alterations. However, our sizing guides can assist you in finding the best fit.
